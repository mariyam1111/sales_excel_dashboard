# Sales Performance Dashboard – Excel Project

## Overview
This project showcases an interactive **Excel dashboard** designed to analyze company-wide sales data and provide actionable business insights. Using Excel’s PivotTables, slicers, and charts, raw data was transformed into a visual and interactive format to highlight key trends, top products, and team performance.

## Key Insights

### Regional Performance
- **West region** leads with **30%** of total sales, followed by **East (27%)**.
- **South (22%)** and **North (21%)** regions show growth potential and could benefit from targeted marketing strategies.

### Product Insights
- **Sneakers** and **Tents** are the **top-selling products**, generating the highest revenue.
- **Blenders** deliver strong profitability despite lower units sold — a **high-value product segment**.
- **Moisturizers** and **Smartphones** show balanced performance but could benefit from promotional campaigns.

### Sales Team Performance
- **Carolyn** and **Grace** lead in sales contributions, showing consistent high performance.
- New reps like **Megan** and **Nicholas** have potential for skill development and future growth.

## Tools & Features Used
- Microsoft Excel
- PivotTables
- Slicers
- Charts & Conditional Formatting
- Interactive dashboards for data-driven decision making

## Key Takeaways
- The dashboard enables quick understanding of sales trends, top products, and regional performance.
- Highlights opportunities for revenue growth and team development.
- Demonstrates the power of **data visualization in Excel** for actionable insights.
